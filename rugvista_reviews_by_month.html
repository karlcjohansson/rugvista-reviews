Monthly Review Volume — 2021 to Mar 2026

Sharp spike in Nov 2025 → Jan 2026 (552 reviews in Jan, ~5× the historical monthly average). Consistent with an aggressive review-solicitation push.

Rating Distribution by Year — the standout finding

5★ share fell from 87.1% (2023) to 78.9% (2025). 1★ ratings tripled over the same period (1.9% → 5.7%).

⚠ Quality signal: Average rating dropped from 4.78 in 2023 → 4.53 in 2025 (−0.25 ★). The distribution shift is real and persists into 2026 YTD (4.60).

Quality / Delivery / Value / Customer Service Over Time

All four sub-ratings declined in sync from 2023 → 2025. Quality fell furthest (−0.27), Customer Service held up best but still dropped −0.23.

🔍 Diagnostic view: The decline isn't isolated to one function — it's across product, logistics, pricing and support. Suggests systemic issues (e.g., broader catalog sourcing, fulfilment partner, or post-solicitation audience shift) rather than a single cause.

Rating Decline by Market — 2023 vs 2025

Swedish customers collapsed from 4.72 → 2.95 (only 20 reviews in 2025, but striking). Polish (−0.46), Portuguese (−0.39), French (−0.35), Italian (−0.32) also cooled sharply.

🇸🇪 Home-market signal: Swedish reviews dropped to a 2.95 avg in 2025. Low volume (n=20) but worth investigating — could indicate an SE-specific issue or a biased solicitation sample.

Review Concentration (Pareto) — long-tail catalog

Just 3.4% of the catalog drives half of all reviews. Top product alone has 1,223 reviews (8.2% of total).

Day-of-Week Pattern

Reviews peak Thu–Fri, drop sharply on Sun/Mon.

Product Line: RVD vs CVD

CVD (4.76 ★) rates higher than RVD (4.67 ★). CVD = classic/vintage designs; RVD = standard Rugvista designs.

Geographic Mix (by review language)

Pan-European customer base. Norwegian (6.3%) and Portuguese (6.0%) customers submit the highest share of 1–2★ reviews; Danish (2.1%) and Spanish (2.6%) the lowest.

Storefront Health by Domain — low-rating share

Review locale (domain the customer purchased from). Austrian (10.6%) and Swedish (9.7%) storefronts show the most dissatisfaction — meaningfully worse than the dominant rugvista.com (2.6%).

🏠 Sweden is the home market but shows unhappy customers. rugvista.se has just 195 reviews in the dataset (1.3% of total) yet 9.7% are 1–2★ — roughly 4× the .com rate. rugvista.at (10.6%) is small but similarly skewed.

Products Needing Attention — lowest-rated with ≥20 reviews

These products drag down site average (4.71). Worth investigating for quality, sizing, or photo-vs-reality issues.
